Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup raisins
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon caraway seeds
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 ¾ cups buttermilk
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.

  2. Step 2

    3. Mix flour, raisins, 1/2 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar, caraway seeds, baking powder, salt, and baking soda in a large bowl.

  3. step 3

    4. Whisk buttermilk and egg together in a small bowl. Stir into flour mixture. Fold butter into the batter.

  4. step 4

    5. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin tin.

  5. step 5

    6. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 30 minutes.
